My Story

I am Mahendra Mannem, a passionate software developer and data enthusiast with a strong commitment to innovation and problem-solving. I graduated in 2024 with a degree in Information Technology from Gudlavalleru Engineering College. My academic journey has equipped me with a robust foundation in IT, complemented by hands-on experience in database management, software development, and web technologies. Throughout my career, I have worked on a variety of projects that demonstrate my expertise in full-stack development. Notably, I designed a scalable and user-friendly Myntra Clone using HTML and CSS for the front-end, showcasing my ability to craft intuitive user interfaces. In addition, I developed several Python-based solutions, including an OTP Email Sender and a Food Ordering System that seamlessly integrates Flask, Razorpay, MySQL, and modern front-end technologies. One of my key projects, the Quote Generator, is built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AJAX, and the DOM. This project dynamically delivers inspiring quotes to users, reflecting my ability to integrate asynchronous data fetching for a seamless user experience. I also created a To-Do List web application with OTP Verification. In this project, users enter their name and email to receive an OTP for verification and then access a to-do list to manage tasks. After completing development, I hosted the app on Vercel, making it publicly accessible. In addition to my project work, I completed a 6-month internship in Python Full Stack development at Codegnan, where I refined my skills in both backend and frontend technologies. This internship has been a pivotal experience in shaping my technical abilities and reinforcing my passion for software development.
